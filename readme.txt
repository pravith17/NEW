CORE FEATURES:  
1. Authentication & Roles:  
   - Participant login before registration/check-in.  
   - Organizer/Admin secure login.  
   - Volunteer role (scan only).  
   - Super-admin role to manage all events and users.  
   - OAuth support (Google, Facebook login).  

2. Event Management:  
   - Organizers create/manage multiple events (title, description, date, capacity, location, banner image).  
   - Event categories (tech, cultural, sports, music).  
   - Event search and filtering by category/date.  
   - Event detail page with countdown timer, capacity status.  

3. Registration + QR:  
   - Registration form with participant profile (name, email, phone, photo upload).  
   - Unique QR generated per registration.  
   - QR emailed immediately.  
   - Email + push notification reminders before event.  
   - Group/team registrations supported.  
   - Paid ticket support (optional toggle for future).  

4. Check-In System:  
   - Check-in via QR scan (camera) or QR upload from gallery.  
   - One-time-use QR (prevent reuse).  
   - Offline mode: cache check-ins, sync later when online.  
   - Instant success/failure animation on scan.  
   - Confetti/fireworks on successful check-in.  

5. Organizer Dashboard:  
   - Real-time live stats of registrations vs check-ins.  
   - Charts & graphs (pie, bar, line) of attendance trends.  
   - Export reports in CSV, Excel, and PDF.  
   - Advanced filters: by event, date, participant type.  
   - Search participants with autocomplete.  
   - Attendance heatmap (peak check-in times).  

6. Notifications:  
   - Email confirmations with QR.  
   - Reminder emails 24 hours + 1 hour before event.  
   - SMS/WhatsApp integration (optional).  
   - Welcome email after check-in.  

7. Gamification & Fun:  
   - Badges for first 100 check-ins, top attendees, etc.  
   - Leaderboard by earliest check-in.  
   - Streaks for participants attending multiple events.  
   - Lucky draw/random winner selector for checked-in participants.  

8. Creative UI/UX + Animations:

   - Floating neon particles in background.  
   - Smooth animated page transitions.  
   - Buttons with ripple + glow hover effects.  
   - QR card flip animation.  
   - Event countdown with pulsing glow.  
   - Progress bar fill animation for event capacity.  
   - Confetti/fireworks after check-in success.  
   - Animated badges unlock effect.  
   - Neon animated loading screen with Pravi Managers logo.  

9. Extra Features (for product completeness):
